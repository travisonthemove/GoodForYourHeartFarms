High Tunnel H1: Green Bean & Community Tunnel

Welcome to our High Tunnel H1 at Good For Your Heart Farms, where green beans flourish and the community meets innovation. Funded by an NRCS grant in 2020, this tunnel is not just a place for growing produce—it's a story of sustainable practices and collaboration.

About the Tunnel

This 90×30 foot high tunnel is designed to optimize growth for our green beans while providing an engaging experience for visitors. With features tailored for efficient production and sustainability, H1 is a hub of innovation and community spirit.

Key Features

  • Drip Watering System: Efficient watering to support our crops.
  • Gutter Water Collection: Water is collected into three 300-gallon tanks.
  • Solar Fans: Powered ventilation ensuring a healthy growing environment.
  • Ground Fabric: Prevents weeds and conserves soil moisture.
  • T-Pole Support & Plastic Trellising: For optimal plant support and structure.

All these elements combine to create a sustainable and efficient space where green beans and community connections thrive.
